Symptoms may appear 2-14 days after exposure to the virus. People with these symptoms may have COVID-19:

Fever or Chills
Wear Masks

Mask can help individuals cover a cough or sneeze, reducing the spread of germs and COVID-19.

Wash Your Hands

Consistently wash your hands with soap to remove pathogens on your hands. Scrub for at least 20 seconds.
